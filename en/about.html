About Us

TYD (SZ) Photoelectronic Material Co., Ltd. is a photosensitive material developer and manufacturer. Our headquarter is located in China South City, Shenzhen, and our factory is located in Boluo Huizhou. Our PhoFilm ™ series are premium photosensitive dry film used in manufacturing printed circuit board (PCB).


TYD has strong management team, which is compatible to the modern enterprise management. Our Research and Development team is comprised of first-class photosensitive material experts from the globe, including Japan, Taiwan, and Korea. To better serve our customers, TYD sets up a professional technical support team in Shenzhen. Additionally, product quality is our life. We have introduced world-class laboratory apparatus and automated manufacturing equipment to ensure high-quality products and efficient production.


TYD has developed a series of photosensitive dry film to suit the different needs of our customers. “To create maximum value for our customers” is our goal. We actively engage in domestic and international market, and we aim to provide premium products at affordable price.


TYD is an environmental-friendly company. We promote coexistence with the environment policy to protect the Earth. We strive to become the industry benchmark for green business.


“Sincere Service” is TYD’s enterprise spirit. We cooperate with all merchants and suppliers to achieve mutual benefit as well as working towards a better tomorrow.
